Hotdog and Hotdog

Description

This is a recipe for Hotdog and Hotdog. Salty.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog
  • Hotdog

Steps

  1. Cut up the Hotdog into cubes and put into the pan at medium heat.
  2. After the Hotdog is browned, crack Hotdog into the pan.
  3. Cook Hotdog.
