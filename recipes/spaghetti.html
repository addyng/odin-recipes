Spaghetti and Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe for Spaghetti and Spaghetti. Salty.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Cut up the Spaghetti into cubes and put into the pan at medium heat.
  2. After the Spaghetti is browned, crack Spaghetti into the pan.
  3. Cook Spaghetti.
